About

Welcome to Inspiration Forge! This blog is designed to provide creative inspiration for nerds (e.g. game masters), and maybe the occasional muggle. Our podcast is just launching, and we hope to eventually host some random generators for creative inspiration.

Inspiration Forge is an open-source project (with source hosted on GitHub) created by Garrison Benson and powered by Python 3, Django, Postgres, Bootstrap 3, Heroku, Gunicorn, and Amazon AWS.
